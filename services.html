Almaty

Among the interesting and beautiful places in the southern capital is the Panfilov Park, spread around the Zenkovsky Cathedral, one of the few remaining intact monuments of the tsarist period. Its uniqueness lies in the fact that the Cathedral was built of wood without the use of nails.

Zailiyskoye Alatau

The majestic mountain peaks of Kengey-Alatau are one of the most beautiful places in Kazakhstan. Two ridges stretched from west to east from Almaty to Issyk-Kul. Guests of Kazakhstan and fans of mountain extreme sports are attracted by impregnable mountain glaciers, beautiful mountain rivers, charming valleys where nomadic shepherds graze sheep.

Peak Khan-Tengri

The most beautiful and mountain peak not only in Kazakhstan, but in the whole world. Fame for this place was brought by the unusual shape of its peak - an almost perfect pointed pyramid, which, during sunrise and sunset, turns scarlet along with the whole mountain. Since ancient times, people have inhabited this interesting and beautiful place with spirits and surrounded it with legends.

Kolsai lakes

The Kul-Sey or Kolsai lakes are one of the most beautiful places in Kazakhstan, these are three green lakes filled with clear ice water of the river of the same name in the foothills of the Zailiyskiy Alatau at an altitude of 2000 m. in the surrounding mountains you can meet deer, mountain goats and even bears.

Aktau

The surroundings of Aktau are famous for their beautiful and unique desert landscapes of the Mangyshlak plateau. The desert climate and erosion have taken their toll on the hard rock formations of the site, creating thousands of miniature mountain ranges, tiny canyons and deep depressions filled with very salty water.

Lake Balhash

This lake is a unique natural object on the territory of Kazakhstan. Its area is huge at a very shallow depth, the eastern half of the lake contains salt water, and the western part is relatively fresh, so the underwater world and the inhabitants of different parts of this beautiful and original place are radically different.

Lake Kaindy

Lake Kaindy is one of the most beautiful places in Kazakhstan. It is located in the Kengei-Alatau gorge and was formed in 1910 as a result of an earthquake. Landslides blocked the mountain gorge, which was filled with spring water, which drowned the living coniferous forest.
